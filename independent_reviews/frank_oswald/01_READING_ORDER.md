## Contamination control

Before submitting the first evaluation, do not read:

- development discussions;
- pull-request commentary about the doctrine;
- informal author explanations;
- promotional descriptions; or
- another reviewer's findings.

Procedural access questions may be raised. Substantive interpretive uncertainty should be recorded as a review finding rather than resolved through author coaching.
