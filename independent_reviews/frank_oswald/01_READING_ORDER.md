# Controlled Reading Order

Read only the frozen materials supplied in `review_materials/`, in the sequence below.

## Stage 1 — Orientation

1. Project overview or executive summary
2. Research charter, scope, and non-claims
3. Terminology and definitions register

**Gate:** The reviewer can state what the project claims, does not claim, and what decision problem it addresses.

## Stage 2 — Constitutional framework

4. Core governance or admissibility framework
5. Authority boundaries and role separation
6. Silence, refusal, petrification, or safe-state logic

**Gate:** The reviewer can identify who or what is permitted to infer, decide, execute, refuse, and recover.

## Stage 3 — Method

7. Observation and evidence model
8. Identifiability specification
9. Constraint and admissibility rules
10. Experiment or validation protocol
11. Failure and recovery criteria

**Gate:** Inputs, transformations, thresholds, and outputs are independently identifiable.

## Stage 4 — Verification

12. Test cases, counterexamples, or simulations
13. Provenance, hashes, release tag, and change record
14. Limitations and unresolved questions

**Gate:** The reviewer can distinguish implemented evidence from conceptual architecture.

## Stage 5 — Evaluation

15. Complete `02_REVIEWER_INDEPENDENCE_DECLARATION.md`
16. Answer `03_REVIEW_QUESTIONS.md`
17. Submit using `04_REVIEW_SUBMISSION_TEMPLATE.md`

## Contamination control

Do not read development discussions, promotional descriptions, informal explanations, or other reviewers' submissions before completing the first independent evaluation.
