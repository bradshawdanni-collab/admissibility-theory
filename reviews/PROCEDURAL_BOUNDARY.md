# Procedural Boundary — Independent Review

The review handoff controls procedure only.

It may define:

- what frozen repository snapshot is reviewed;
- reading order;
- reviewer independence disclosures;
- submission mechanics;
- preservation and provenance rules;
- separation between reviewer evidence and author response.

It may not:

- reinterpret doctrine for the reviewer;
- pre-answer review questions;
- steer the reviewer toward approval or rejection;
- alter the frozen doctrine object;
- treat reviewer participation as endorsement;
- treat review acceptance as agreement;
- suppress ambiguity, disagreement, or negative findings.

Any substantive author explanation supplied before review submission should be disclosed in the review record because it may affect independence.
