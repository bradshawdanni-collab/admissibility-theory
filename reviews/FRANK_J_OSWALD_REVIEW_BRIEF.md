## Questions the review must answer

### A. Framework identity

- Is the framework clearly an admission or entry-condition framework, rather than a hidden ranking or truth-adjudication system?
- Is that distinction maintained consistently across the repository?

### B. Axiom quality

For each of the four axioms:

- Is it clearly defined?
- Is it necessary for the framework's stated purpose?
- Is it independently testable?
- Is it redundant with another axiom?
- Can you identify a counterexample or edge case?

Then assess whether the four axioms are jointly sufficient for the stated admission function.

### C. Novelty and distinction

- Is the proposed discriminability requirement materially stricter than ordinary falsifiability?
- Is the distinction demonstrated, or mainly asserted?
- Which existing philosophical, scientific, legal, or engineering concepts appear closest to the framework?

A literature review is welcome but not required. Unsupported novelty claims should be flagged.

### D. Operational reproducibility

- Can the six-question doctrine be applied without author interpretation?
- Could two competent reviewers reasonably classify the same claim differently while following the same procedure?
- Where could divergence enter the reasoning chain?
- Does the template capture those divergences adequately?

### E. Scope and boundary stability

- Does `CIL-000` successfully stop the framework at the admission gate?
- Do any examples or claims cross that boundary?
- Are there domains where the boundary becomes unclear or impractical?

### F. Evidence and maturity

- Are the repository's maturity labels accurate?
- Which claims are conceptual, formal, empirically supported, or not yet demonstrated?
- Does the current evidence justify any stronger status than the repository claims?

### G. Material disagreement

State at least one of the following explicitly:

- the most important point on which you disagree;
- the most serious ambiguity you found;
- the strongest counterexample you can construct; or
- why, after attempting to find one, you found no material disagreement.

Do not manufacture disagreement merely to satisfy the form. The reasoning path matters more than the outcome.
